What is I'm Running Live?

I'm Running Live is a revolutionary platform that bridges the gap between runners and their supporters during marathons and races. Whether you're running your first 5K or tackling the Berlin Marathon, our platform ensures your loved ones can follow every step of your journey and send you encouragement exactly when you need it most.

How It Works

1

Runner Starts

Download our mobile app, create your race, and start tracking when your event begins.

2

Share Your Link

Get a unique tracking URL to share with family and friends before your race.

3

Live Tracking

Supporters follow your real-time location, pace, distance, and race statistics on an interactive map.

4

Real-Time Cheers

Supporters send encouraging messages that are instantly read aloud through your phone as you run.
